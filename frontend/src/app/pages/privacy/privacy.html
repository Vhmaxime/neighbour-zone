Privacy Policy

This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and what your rights are.

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. What we collect

  • Account data: username, first name, last name, email, phone number.
  • Profile data: bio (only if you add it).
  • App content: posts, events, marketplace items, and friend connections you create.
  • Security data: your password is stored as a secure hash (never as plain text).

2. Why we use your data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your profile and content to other users where applicable.
  • To enable features like friends, posts, events, and marketplace.
  • To keep the platform secure and prevent abuse.

3. Who can see your data

Some profile information may be visible to other users depending on the feature (e.g., posts/events show the author/organizer). Your email and phone number should only be used for account and contact-related functionality.

4. Data retention

You can delete your account in the Settings page. When you delete your account, your data will be removed according to the platform’s deletion behavior.

5. Your rights

  • Access: you can view your own account data in your profile.
  • Correction: you can edit your profile details.
  • Deletion: you can delete your account.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us via the support channel of the platform.
