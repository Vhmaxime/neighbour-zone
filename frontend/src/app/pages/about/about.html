About Neighbour Zone

Neighbour Zone is a local community platform designed to bring neighbours closer together.

In a time where people often live next to each other without really connecting, Neighbour Zone makes it easy to discover who lives nearby, share items, organize local events and help one another in a trusted environment.

What you can do on Neighbour Zone

  • Connect with neighbours
    Get to know the people in your area and build real local connections.
  • Create and join events
    From neighbourhood meetups to small activities, see what’s happening around you.
  • Share and borrow items
    Offer items you don’t use often or request something you need from nearby neighbours.
  • Stay in control of your privacy
    You decide what information you share and with whom.

Our goal

Neighbour Zone aims to strengthen local communities by making neighbourhood interaction simple, safe and accessible for everyone.

We believe that strong neighbourhoods start with small connections.
